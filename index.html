Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Neko Name Wheel?

Simply type or paste names into the text area, one name per line. Ensure you have between 3 and 50 names. Then, click the 'Spin Wheel' button and watch our lucky Neko choose a winner for you!

Is this name picker really random and fair?

Yes! The Neko Name Wheel uses a robust randomization algorithm to ensure every name has an equal chance of being selected. It's designed for fair and unbiased selections every time.

Can I save my wheel for later?

Your names are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. This means if you close the tab or browser, your list of names will still be there when you return.

How do I share my wheel with others?

After entering your names, a 'Share Wheel' button will appear. Click it to copy a unique link to your clipboard. When others open this link, your names will automatically populate their wheel, ready to spin!

Is Neko Name Wheel free to use?

Absolutely! Neko Name Wheel is completely free to use, with no hidden costs, sign-ups, or downloads required. Enjoy unlimited spins and fair selections.

What is a Maneki-neko (lucky cat)?

A Maneki-neko, or lucky cat, is a common Japanese figurine (ceramic or plastic) which is often believed to bring good luck to the owner. Our adorable Neko character embodies this spirit, bringing fortune to your random selections!

Can I use this for classroom or giveaways?

Yes, Neko Name Wheel is perfect for classrooms, giveaways, raffles, or any situation where you need a fair and fun way to pick a random name. Teachers, streamers, event organizers, and anyone needing a reliable random name picker will find it incredibly useful.
